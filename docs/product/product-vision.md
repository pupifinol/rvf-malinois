# RVF Malinois Product Vision

RVF Malinois is an industrial operational monitoring platform for portable multiphase Well Testing operations developed by RVF Soluciones Energéticas C.A.

The platform provides:

- realtime telemetry monitoring
- historical production analysis
- alarm visualization
- operational intelligence
- commissioning workflows
- equipment catalog management
- client visibility portals

The platform is focused on:

- operational reliability
- industrial simplicity
- historical traceability
- multi-client operations
- scalable architecture
- future AI-assisted analytics
